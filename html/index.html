When did Tea first appear in Bangladesh?

In Bangladesh, tea cultivation first started near the Chittagong Club in the 1840s. Malnicherra in Sylhet is the first tea garden of Bangladesh established in 1854 and in commercial production in 1857. (Source:Tea Traders Association of Bangladesh)
